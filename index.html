Process Service

Process Services are necessary when you need someone notified of a lawsuit, court action, restraining order, or other legal matters. This can be a quick and simple process, in some cases taken care of the same day.

Services

We offer process serving for the Albuquerque area, we can do same-day service for an extra cost. We will make 3 attempts at one address for each service needed. It is additional for each defendant and address that you need served. Feel free to contact us for more information, as well as information for pricing or discounts for ongoing contracts.

About Us

We are a locally owned and operated business. We operate and offer services in the greater Albuquerque area for your process serving needs. Our goal is to make this process as fast and simple for you as possible. We know this can be a stressful time, and we take pride in handling your case with the utmost efficiency, speed, and care in the business.
